## Response

Insomnia is a sleep disorder characterized by difficulty falling asleep, staying asleep, or waking too early without being able to return to sleep. Common causes include psychological stress and irregular sleep schedules. It can lead to impaired concentration and daytime fatigue, which may affect work performance and overall well-being. Effective management involves identifying underlying causes and improving sleep hygiene, with medical treatment considered in persistent cases.
