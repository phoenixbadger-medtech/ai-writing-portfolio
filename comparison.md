# Comparison Task

## Prompt
Explain hypertension in simple terms.

---

## Response A
Hypertension is when your blood pressure is too high. It can cause health problems and you may need medication or lifestyle changes.

---

## Response B
Hypertension (high blood pressure) is a condition in which the force of blood against artery walls remains consistently elevated. Over time, it increases the risk of heart disease, stroke, and kidney damage. Management includes lifestyle changes such as diet and exercise, and medication when necessary.

---

## Evaluation

Response B is superior to Response A.

### Reasons

- **Clarity:** Response B defines the condition precisely; Response A is vague (“too high” lacks meaning).
- **Completeness:** Response B includes risks and management; Response A is minimal and incomplete.
- **Tone:** Response B maintains a professional, informative tone; Response A is overly simplistic.
- **Structure:** Response B is logically organized; Response A lacks depth and flow.

---

## Conclusion

Response B is more accurate, structured, and informative, making it more suitable for training AI systems.
